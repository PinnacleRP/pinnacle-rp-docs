# 📈 Gang Progression

Progression within the Pinnacle Roleplay criminal ecosystem is earned, not given. Crews must demonstrate consistency, high-quality roleplay, and impact to move up the ranks.

## Crew Application Process

1. Prospect Crew: Start with a roster of 6 members. This period lasts 3-10 days and provides no official recognition.
2. Trial Crew: After the prospect period, crews may be promoted.

## Crew Ranks and Privileges

Prospect Crew:

- Hostile cap: 8
- Roster cap: 10

Trial Crew:

- Hostile cap: 10
- Roster cap: 14
- Full access to gang events

Crew:

- Hostile cap: 14
- Roster cap: 18
- 4 custom clothing pieces
- Gang storage access, boss menu, official turf

Organisation:

- Hostile cap: 18
- Roster cap: 22
- 8 custom clothing pieces
- Custom MLO, garage, black market access, optional 4-postal turf

Syndicated:

- Hostile cap: 22
- Roster cap: 28
- 10 custom clothing pieces
- Larger stash, gang helicopter, logo spray, optional 8-postal turf

Empire:

- Hostile cap: 28
- Roster cap: 32
- 13 custom clothing pieces
- Larger stash, custom gang vehicle, hall of fame enshrinement

## Turf Rules

- Crews may claim as much turf as they want through RP, but only 4-postal turf is granted on the tablet.
- You can press, hands, or rob anyone in your turf if they are actively chilling there.
- Turf can only be challenged by crews at Trial level or lower.
