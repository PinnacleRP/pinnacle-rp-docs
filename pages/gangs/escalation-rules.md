# 🚦 Conflict Boundaries and Carryover

## Hostile Beef Carryover Rules

Hostile actions may not carry over from:

- Cayo Perico Island KOS
- Official Gang Events
- Crate Drop KOS
- Hold the Zone KOS
- Vendor Claiming

Any hostility or retaliation from the scenarios above must end once the event or KOS period concludes.

## Robbing Rival Gang Members

When robbing a rival gang member, you may take anything on their person except clean money and business assets. You still may not rob a dead body.
