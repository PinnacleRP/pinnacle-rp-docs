# 🧩 Business Tier System

## Food Business Tier System
A progression system designed specifically for food businesses, rewarding activity, growth, and quality RP.

Trial Tier - Foundation License

Your starting business tier includes all standard features provided under server business rules.

Unlocks:
- Access to all basic business functions
- Standard 4 approved food/drink items
- Access to menu prop
- Basic storage access
- Basic 4 business clothing/uniforms each for male and female
- Ability to operate under all normal business rules

Purpose: Gives every approved food business the essentials to operate before progressing.

Tier 1 - Starter Kitchen

Get your business established.

Unlocks:
- 2% tax reduction
- Access to 2 extra food/drink items
- Small ingredient storage increase
- Access to food delivery job
- Access to a music box

Purpose: Expands your menu and helps build your brand.

Tier 2 - Licensed Kitchen

Your business is expanding operations.

Unlocks:
- 4% tax reduction
- Ability to cook/prep items
- Full ingredient list and collection system
- Access to 2 extra business clothing/uniforms each for male and female
- Medium storage increase

Purpose: Unlocks full kitchen operations and improved customization.

Tier 3 - Signature Brand

Your business becomes recognised across the city.

Unlocks:
- 6% tax reduction
- Custom business item (healing item, combo meal, signature dish, etc.)
- Access to 2 extra food/drink items
- Access to 2 extra business clothing/uniforms each for male and female
- Large storage increase
- Business advertisement package (TBC)

Purpose: Gives your business a unique identity and premium offerings.

Tier 4 - Culinary Empire

The highest level of food business ownership.

Unlocks:
- 8% tax reduction
- Specialty vehicle unlock (food truck, catering van, mobile kitchen, branded delivery vehicle)
- Ability to run slight rotating menu options
- Priority support for approved updates
- Legacy/elite business recognition in Discord

Purpose: Endgame prestige with exclusive content and maximum business status.
