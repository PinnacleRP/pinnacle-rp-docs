# 🛡️ Anti-Cheat and Exploits

## Cheating, Hacking, and Exploiting

Utilising third-party programs to gain undue advantages is forbidden. This includes cheating (hacking), exploiting undiscovered glitches, duplicating weapons/items, use of macros or misusing commands to identify players.

Mod packs that remove bushes, props or keep the time of the day at a certain time, or mod packs that enhance the sound of footsteps are prohibited.

All offenders caught cheating, executing or hacking will receive a permanent, non-appealable ban.

## 💸 Real Money Trade (RMT)

Trading, buying, selling, or gifting in-game items, weapons, vehicles, or currency in exchange for real-world money or any other form of currency, item or service is strictly prohibited. Offenders will receive a permanent, non-appealable ban.
