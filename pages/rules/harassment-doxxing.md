# 🤝 Respect, Harassment, and Hate Speech

## Respect and Inclusivity

We welcome people of all backgrounds and expect all members to treat each other with respect both In-Character (IC) and out-of-character (OOC). You must not attack, demean, or promote hatred or violence against anyone based on the following:

- Age
- Color or race
- Ethnicity
- Gender or gender identity
- National origin or immigration status
- Religious affiliation or beliefs
- Disability (mental or physical)
- Sex or sexual orientation
- Socioeconomic class, status, or caste
- Weight, size, or body type
- Military service

## Roleplay Boundaries

All actions and comments must remain within reasonable roleplay context and must not be used as a cover for personal attacks. This includes:

- Sexual harassment, bullying, excessive name-calling, or shaming.
- Remarks about a players OOC persona or real life
- Using derogatory language or slurs towards a downed character or over a downed body regardless of RP context
- No use of any OOC information about anything towards anyone
- No talking over a downed character or over a downed body no matter what
- Rape roleplay will not be allowed, this will result in a instant ban
- Accusations or discussions relating to pedophilia, IC or OOC, will not be allowed and will result in a ban

## Slurs and Hate Speech

The use of racial, sexual or any other discriminatory slurs is strictly prohibited, both IC and OOC. Use of these will result in immediate disciplinary actions. This includes:

- Verbal, written or visual attacks (slurs, insults, stereotypes)
- Jokes, memes or roleplay intended to ridicule protected groups
- Threats, harassment or incitement of violence based on protected characteristics
- Promoting extremist ideologies or conspiracy theories that encourage violence against others
