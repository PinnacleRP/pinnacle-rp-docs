# 🤝 Respect, Harassment, and Hate Speech

## Respect and Inclusivity

We welcome people of all backgrounds and expect all members to treat each other with respect both in-character (IC) and out-of-character (OOC). You must not attack, demean, or promote hatred or violence against anyone based on:

- Age
- Color or race
- Ethnicity
- Gender or gender identity
- National origin or immigration status
- Religious affiliation or beliefs
- Disability (mental or physical)
- Sex or sexual orientation
- Socioeconomic class, status, or caste
- Weight, size, or body type
- Military service

## Roleplay Boundaries

All actions and comments must remain within reasonable roleplay context and must not be used as a cover for personal attacks. This includes:

- Sexual harassment, bullying, excessive name-calling, or shaming
- Remarks about a player's OOC persona or real life
- Using derogatory language toward a downed character or over a downed body
- Talking over a downed character or over a downed body
- Rape roleplay (instant ban)
- Accusations or discussions relating to pedophilia, IC or OOC (ban)

## Slurs and Hate Speech

The use of racial, sexual, or any other discriminatory slurs is strictly prohibited, IC or OOC. This includes:

- Verbal, written, or visual attacks (slurs, insults, stereotypes)
- Jokes, memes, or roleplay intended to ridicule protected groups
- Threats, harassment, or incitement of violence based on protected characteristics
- Promoting extremist ideologies or conspiracy theories that encourage violence
