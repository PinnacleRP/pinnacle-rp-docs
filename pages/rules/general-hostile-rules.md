# 🔥 Hostile Roleplay Rules

## Safe Zones

Designated areas and all officially recognized businesses are safe zones. Hostile roleplay, shooting, or ramming is not allowed within these areas. It is also forbidden to start a hostile interaction and then seek refuge in a safe zone or transport a player out of a safe zone to begin hostilities.

## Hostile Roleplay Restrictions

- If you are not part of a crew/gang/org, hostile RP is limited to 8 participants.
- Robberies and heists allow up to 8 participants (3 external max) and only 1 vehicle for the initial escape.
- Emergency services (Ambulance, Fire, Police) may not be robbed for items.
- Police hostages are allowed only with valid RP reasons. Ambulance and Fire personnel are off limits.

## Fear RP / Valuing Your Life

- If a gun is on you and you are unarmed, surrender.
- Do not intentionally crash or act recklessly in dangerous situations.
- During robberies or when held at gunpoint, prioritize safety and comply.

## Robbing and Scamming

- Robbing is allowed only after meaningful interaction.
- You may not rob for clean money or whitelist job items.
- Robbing dead bodies is not allowed.
- Scamming is not allowed.

## Baiting

Intentionally provoking police or criminal organizations to force a conflict without meaningful RP is prohibited.

## Combat Logging

Disconnecting to avoid RP is prohibited. After starting or engaging in hostile activity, you must remain in the server for at least 10 minutes, even if you evade police.

## Hostile RP Guidelines

- Hostile RP cannot be initiated on players engaged in legal activities without meaningful interaction.
- Red Zones are KOS areas.
- Helmets are not allowed in hostilities (LSPD exempt).
- Boot-checking is not permitted unless meaningful RP occurred.
- Killing someone and robbing them is not allowed, except on Cayo during KOS times.
- No hostility may be initiated 30 minutes before server restart.

## Heist Rules

- Heists allow a maximum of 8 participants (3 externals max).
- Only 1 car is allowed for the initial escape from the heist area.
- Third-partying another group's heist is not allowed.

## Combat Rules

Tripping is not allowed in combat unless it is to prevent a player from fleeing or to escape pursuit. Tripping to execute someone is forbidden.

## Police Interaction Rules

- Convoys or prisoner transports may be intercepted if proper declaration was made.
- Players may not camp outside PD to initiate a scene as soon as officers arrive.
- Ramming is prohibited.

## Cayo Perico Island

Cayo is a KOS zone during set times when a red circle is active. Any hostile on Cayo cannot be brought to mainland.
