# 🚫 Prohibited Content

## Intellectual Property

You must **not upload, send, share, or display any content that breaches the FiveM Terms of Service.** This includes any use of **copyrighted or trademarked material** (e.g., real-world brands, TV shows, movies, logos, celebrity names or likenesses) in **any form or platform within the server**, such as:

- Phone apps like SMS, Birdy, Instapic or Photos**
- Phone contact images, profile photos or uploaded media
- Contract profile images or any media embedded in contracts
- Advertisements (yellow pages, etc)
- /me commands or any in-character actions referencing copyrighted content

These types of content violate FiveM’s Terms of Service. Anyone found uploading or distributing such material may face content removal, warnings, or **permanent bans.**

## Real-World Criminal Organizations

Any depiction, reference, roleplay, glorification, or support of real-world criminal organisations, gangs, militias, extremist organisations, terrorist groups, or hate groups/ideologies is strictly prohibited in all forms.

This includes, but is not limited to:

- Using real-world names, aliases, slang, symbols, flags, logos, colours, numbers, or other identifiers
- Roleplaying as, or in any way portraying yourself as, a member, associate, or supporter of such groups
- Glorifying, endorsing, romanticising, or attempting to legitimise their actions, beliefs, or history through roleplay, dialogue, clothing, tattoos, liveries, props, or events
- Creating, distributing, or displaying in-game assets or media (including streams, videos, or images) containing references to these groups
- Using slogans, chants, gestures, music, graffiti, or coded language associated with these groups
- Promoting extremist ideologies or conspiracy theories that encourage, incite, or justify violence against others
- Depicting, promoting, or supporting violent extremism, terrorism, or the perpetrators of such acts

Offenders will receive a permanent, non-appealable ban.
